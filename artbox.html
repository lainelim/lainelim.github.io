Artbox 2017

Design Challenge

ARTBOX held its first Singapore flea market in Apr 2017. Our jewellery brand, Lovegem Co and lifestyle brand Jou Mourie jointly participated in the event.

An attractive and functional booth layout was required to attract and manage visitors, raise brand awareness and ultimately, achieve sales over the 3 days.

Mockup of booth layout

Prior to the event, visits to similar flea markets were made in order to observe how flea market booths were designed to stand out, and to attract crowds to take a closer look at the products.

Mock ups of the booth were made to visualize the operations and layout for the event.

Artbox: Day One

We encountered several issues on the booth layout, leading to a $50 sale for the whole day:

  • Customers' mental model of jewellery did not match the booth's display -- while earrings came in a pair, the booth displayed them singly for ease of trying
  • Lighting was too dim to attract the crowds who usually come after dark
  • Design of certain trendy items were too new; customers could not relate and visualize the pieces on themselves

We iterated by changing the display for earrings to match customers' mental model, added a floodlight for better visibility at night, and added a digital catalogue with modelling pictures to illustrate wear of certain trend pieces.

Day 2

We saw the following results:

  • Large increase in the number of visitors to the booth
  • 10-fold increase in revenue
  • Sale of earrings made up 30% of total revenue for the day

The same display on the third day yielded similar results as the second, with a slight increase in total revenue due to an earlier crowd on Sunday.
